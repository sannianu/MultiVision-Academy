About Us

Name: MultiVision Academy

Mission Statement:
Towards potentials discoveries and development for the virtues and human capital development.

Vision Statement:
To be a world class citadel, where transformers of spiritual and economical society will be produced for global consumption

Objectives:

  1. To breed readers that eventually becomes leaders, in both aspects of human life i.e. Spiritual and Economic.
  2. To create an avenue for a boarderless learning opportunity for all.
  3. To be pathfinders for future generations in all aspect of human endeavours.
  4. Breed personalities that will be self-sustained after their learning journey.
  5. To prepare sound mind for the next level of academic challenge, that leads to a greater height.
  6. To maintain moderacy in human culture.
  7. To create balance between Islamic and Western Curriculum, in order to achieve a perfect outcome with Allah's grace.

MultiVision Academy was founded in 2018 (1439 AH). It is an Islamic and Conventional Curriculum Based School which aim to be a world class citadel, where transformers of Spiritual and Economical Society will be produced for global consumption. We have an International Based Standard Curriculum for both Islamic and Conventional Education which range from: Arabic, Adhkar, Islamic Studies, Tahfiz, English Language, Yourba, Other Languages(French), Mathematics
Sciences: (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Mathematics),
Arts & Humanity: (Literature in English, History, Government, Civic Education),
Commerce: (Financial Accounting, Commerce, Office Practice, Book Keeping, Data Processing, Business Studies)
Social Sciences: (Economics, Geography)
Information Communication Technology(e-Learning, Internet, Web Development and Python Programming Language),Vocational Studies, Moral Talks and lots more...

Address: 23, Tunde Dada Street, Victory Estate, Adof Bus Stop, LASU-Igando Road, Ojo, Lagos State, Nigeria.
